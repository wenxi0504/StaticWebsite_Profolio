About me

Web Developer | Front End Developer

As to academic projects, I contributed to the development of two full-stack web applications. Additionally, I successfully developed front-end web applications using technologies such as bootstrap and REACT, demonstrating proficiency in web interface design.

Moreover, I have consistently met the challenges presented in this field with over four years of experience as an IT project coordinator. My attention to detail, organizational skills and strong communication abilities have allowed me to lead software development teams and ensure the success of each project assigned to me. While performing this role, I successfully managed over a dozen small-scale projects independently and collaborated in managing national-level projects valued at 20 million dollars.
